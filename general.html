Canadas National food

Poutine

Poutine, a Canadian culinary icon, is a dish that embodies the country's love for comfort food and indulgence. This delectable creation consists of crispy french fries, smothered in a rich and savory gravy, and topped with squeaky cheese curds. The contrasting textures – the crisp fries, the creamy gravy, and the chewy cheese curds – create a symphony of flavors in every bite. Poutine is a dish that transcends generations, bringing families and friends together around a steaming plate of pure deliciousness. Whether enjoyed as a late-night snack or a hearty meal, poutine is a true testament to the Canadian spirit of hearty, unpretentious food.

The prime minister of Canada

Justin Trudeau

The President of Canada is not a position that exists. Canada is a Parliamentary Democracy with a Prime Minister as the head of government. The current Prime Minister of Canada is Justin Trudeau, who has been in office since 2015. He is the 23rd Prime Minister of Canada.
